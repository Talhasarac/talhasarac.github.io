System One Recorder Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

System One Recorder is a local audio recording app.

Data Collection

System One Recorder does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Audio Recordings

Audio recordings are saved locally on your device. The app does not upload recordings to a server. If you choose to share a recording, it is shared only through the iOS share sheet and only with the destination you select.

Microphone Access

The app requests microphone access so it can record audio. Microphone audio is used only to create recordings on your device.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact the app developer through the support information listed on the App Store product page.
